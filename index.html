Rules

  • Deplete opponent's HP to win the round.
  • First to the required wins takes the match.
  • Timer runs out? More HP wins.
  • Special charges passively — gold ring = ready!
  • Grab items that drop from the sky for power-ups!
  • On mobile: joystick to move/jump, buttons to fight.

Items (SSB-style)

  • ⚡ Lightning — Instant 30 dmg + stun to opponent
  • ❤️ Heart — Restore 30 HP
  • 💖 Big Heart — Restore 67 HP
  • 💥 Bomb — Explodes: massive knockback + 40 dmg
  • 💣 Sticky Bomb — Pick it up, then touch your opponent within 3 seconds — they explode! If time runs out, YOU explode instead!
  • ⭐ Star — 5 seconds of invincibility
  • 🔥 Flame — Speed & attack buff for 6 seconds
  • 🧊 Ice — Freezes opponent for 2 seconds
  • 🏹 Arrow — Homing projectile: 25 dmg
